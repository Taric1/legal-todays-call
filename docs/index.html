2. Information the App uses

The App is designed to answer simple weather-related questions for locations you choose.

  • Location. With your permission, the App may access your device location to show weather for where you are. You may also search for places or save favorite locations; those choices are stored on your device (see below).
  • Location search queries. When you search for a place by name, your query is sent to a third-party geocoding service (Open-Meteo Geocoding) to return coordinates and place names.
  • Weather requests. To show forecasts, the App sends the latitude and longitude of the selected location to the Open-Meteo weather API. Open-Meteo processes these requests according to its own policies.
  • Settings and preferences. Thresholds, units, and similar preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • Cached data. Forecast and location data may be cached on your device to improve performance and offline behavior.
  • Purchases. If you buy “Remove Ads,” payment and purchase records are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive your full payment card details.
  • Advertising. If ads are shown, Google AdMob may use identifiers and technical data to deliver and measure ads, including in support of personalized advertising where allowed and consented to. See Section 4.

The App does not require you to create an account. We do not ask for your name, email, or phone number in the App itself for basic use.
